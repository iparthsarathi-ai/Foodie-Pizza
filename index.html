About Us

Your Local Pizza
Café in Lucknow

Foodie Pizza is your go-to neighbourhood pizza café, located in Sarojini Nagar, Lucknow. We serve a wide variety of freshly made vegetarian pizzas in three sizes, along with combos, pasta, garlic bread, burgers, shakes, mojitos and more.

Everything on our menu is 100% vegetarian. Place your order directly on WhatsApp or give us a call — we'll have it ready for you.
